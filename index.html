What is AEO - Answer Engine Optimization?
Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring content so it can be directly extracted and used as an answer by AI search tools and answer engines - including Google AI Overviews, Bing Copilot, and voice assistants. AEO techniques include question-format headings, concise answer blocks, FAQPage schema, and structured data markup that makes your content easy for AI systems to extract and cite.
What is GEO - Generative Engine Optimization?
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the process of optimizing your content and brand presence so that large language models (LLMs) and generative AI platforms - including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini - cite your brand, reference your content, and recommend your business in their AI-generated responses. GEO focuses on brand authority, original research, factual density, and entity recognition.
What is the difference between AEO and GEO?
AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) targets direct answer extraction - getting your content selected as the answer inside Google AI Overviews or Bing Copilot. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) targets brand citation in generative AI responses from platforms like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude. Both are part of what Nagana Media calls AI SEO, and both require different but overlapping optimization strategies.
